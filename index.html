About Serva

Coined from "Server", Serva was created to serve the modern society. As our tagline "here to serve you" goes, Serva exists to serve by bridging the gap between skilled service providers and the people who need them.

Our Vision

To empower SMEs with exposure, excellence, and longevity.

Our Mission

  • To increase visibility and profitability for SMEs within their immediate location.
  • To bring unlimited service options to the fingertips of locals.

Serva addresses three critical challenges in the local service economy: poor visibility for skilled artisans and SMEs, limited access to reliable services for households, and the declining interest in traditional skills among younger generations; thereby supporting SDGs 1, 8, 9, and 10.

For business owners

We showcase your services to a larger audience while partnering with you to raise your business standards, quality, and delivery.

For you

Whether you’ve just relocated, experienced a change in status, unforeseen circumstances, or simply don’t know where to look; we make a wide variety services accessible to you at no extra cost.

For the future

We invest in tomorrow’s leaders through partnerships with service providers to offer both paid and sponsored skills acquisition for youth, ensuring business continuity and the preservation of valuable knowledge.

Our Commitment to You

At Serva, your trust means everything to us. We have put strong systems in place to ensure quality service, safety, transparency, and peace of mind for both service users and service providers.

Ensuring your utmost satisfaction every time.

1. Simple Booking Experience

Our platform is designed to make booking services quick, easy, and stress-free.

2. Secure Payments (Escrow System)

All payments are made directly to Serva, not the service provider, ensuring full protection through our trusted escrow system.

3. Risk-Free Guarantee

A portion of your payment is held as a risk fee and is refundable in cases of verified dissatisfaction.

4. Thoroughly Vetted Professionals

Every service provider is carefully screened and verified using valid, nationally recognized identification and documentation.

5. Pay Only for Satisfaction

Service providers are only paid after you confirm that the job has been completed satisfactorily and to standard.

6. Live Service Tracking

You receive updates throughout the service process, so you’re always informed and in control.

7. Quality Assurance & Supervision

We monitor service delivery to ensure it meets our quality standards.

8. Trained and Improved Professionals

Our service providers undergo regular training to improve their skills, professionalism, and service delivery.

9. Reliable Support System

10. Transparency & No Hidden Charges

What you see is what you pay, no hidden costs, no surprises.
